About Me

My research focuses on black holes as portals to quantum gravity phenomena, particularly exploring how a discrete spacetime structure may address singularities. To this end, I develop effective models inspired by loop quantum gravity. My long-term objective is to deepen our understanding of quantum gravity and provide insights into the fundamental nature of spacetime.

I completed my PhD in loop quantum gravity with the highest honors at the University of the Basque Country in 2023. Presently, I am employed by the French CNRS (Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique) as a postdoctoral researcher at the Centre de Physique Théorique, University of Aix-Marseille. I am also a fellow of the QISS (Quantum Information and Structure of Spacetime) initiative.
